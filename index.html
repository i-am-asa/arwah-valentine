Will you be my valentine, Arwah?

Across the miles that never seem to end,
I count the days until I'm near you again.
Distance is cruel in the quiet of night,
when all I want is your warmth within sight.

Those last ten days we held the world away
were the best I've ever lived, I still replay
your giggles spilling into the air,
your laughter dancing everywhere.

I remember the way your eyes would shine,
your beautiful face so close to mine,
the gentle music in every breath you'd take,
the way my heart would tremble and wake.

Your curves felt like poetry under my hands,
a language only love understands.
And every moment we shared, every embrace,
still lingers in time I cannot replace.

Until I see you again, I'll hold on tight
to memories glowing soft and bright —
because wherever you are, however far,
my everything is exactly where you are.

Forever Yours, Adnan
